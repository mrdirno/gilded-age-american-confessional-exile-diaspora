## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a letter from Elara to her father, one year after her exile, detailing a truth she could never have spoken in Gramercy Park.
- Create a journal entry from Samuel Thorne's perspective, cataloging Elara's 'resources' and her potential value in his network.
- Draft a scene where Elara encounters another exiled American in a European salon, and they perform a silent, mutual assessment of each other's ruin.
- Imagine a chapter from the perspective of the sharp-eyed Frenchwoman in the ship's cabin, speculating on Elara's past and future.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive map tracing Elara's journey from New York to Rotterdam to Haarlem, annotating locations with sensory details (smells, sounds, textures) from the text.
- Create a 'Social Ledger' visualization tool that tracks the fluctuating value of names, favors, and secrets among the exiled characters.
